I. INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND

The present report is submitted pursuant to a request of the GA. The UN has continued to support programs in the region during the reporting period, working closely with UNDP and partners in Vietnam and South Korea.

The Secretary-General wishes to emphasize that the Organization received 5 new mandates in the reporting period, the 3rd such increase in as many years. The total allocation amounted to USD 250,000, of which 40,000 dollars was disbursed in the 1st quarter.
